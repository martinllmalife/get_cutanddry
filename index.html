Upload Adoption Data (CSV)

Upload the Supplier Partner Customer Base Report from Cut+Dry. Export it weekly to track adoption over time. Go to Reports → Supplier Partner Customer Base in your Cut+Dry dashboard and download as CSV.

Upload the Customer Orders Report from Cut+Dry to calculate Customer Placed Order % (CPO) per rep. Go to Reports → Customer Orders and export as Excel or CSV.
